Description

Welcome to Basket Random!

Dive into the chaotic world of Basket Random, a two-player arcade game where unpredictable physics reign supreme. Battle it out on various basketball courts, hopping and spinning in a frenzy to secure the ball. Each successful dunk propels you to the next level, adding to the excitement!

How to Play

Control:
Use the W key to maneuver player one and the up arrow to guide player two. Engage in frantic battles over the basketball, bouncing and spinning through the air in an attempt to score. The team that scores first in each round emerges victorious, and the first player to reach 5 wins claims overall victory!

Random Changes:
After every goal, brace yourself for a whirlwind of surprises as you're transported to new locations with ever-changing physics and attire. Embrace the unpredictability and adapt to the random alterations, making each round a fresh and exhilarating experience!

Features

  • Quirky, bouncy basketball players
  • Randomly changing levels and physics
  • Two-player mode for competitive fun
  • Unique twist on traditional basketball gameplay

Release Date

Basket Random made its debut in April 2020, first on HTML5 platforms and subsequently on Android. Enjoy seamless gaming experiences on both desktop and mobile browsers!

Developer

Developed by RHM Interactive, Basket Random promises endless hours of unpredictable basketball mayhem!

Platforms

Experience Basket Random on web browsers, including Chromebook computers, and enjoy fullscreen mode without any disruptive ads. Additionally, the game is available on Android and iOS devices for gaming on the go!

Controls

Control player one with the W key and player two with the up arrow key.
