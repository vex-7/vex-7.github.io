Description

Join the Adventure in Raft Wars 2

Raft Wars 2, an exhilarating action-packed shooting game, is the brainchild of Martijn Kunst and serves as the sequel to the beloved classic Raft Wars. Join Simon and his brother on their quest as they return from a relaxing holiday only to discover a water park sitting atop their buried treasure. Determined to reclaim what's rightfully theirs, they embark on a mission to shut down the water park and retrieve their gold!

Ruin waterslides, pools, lifeguards, security guards, and more as you strategically navigate through each level to reclaim your treasure. Earn coins by making precise shots and unlock powerful weapons such as grenades, rockets, and raft upgrades to aid you in your quest.

How to Play

Engage in the action with these simple controls:

  • Mouse - Click and draw to shoot

Seamless Browser Experience

Experience uninterrupted gameplay in Raft Wars 2 right from your web browser, optimized especially for Chromebook computers. Enjoy full-screen mode and dive into the adventure without any pesky ads disrupting your gameplay!

About the Creators

Raft Wars 2 is the creative masterpiece of Martijn Kunst and TinyDobbins, based in the Netherlands. Get ready to embark on an epic adventure and reclaim your treasure in this thrilling shooting game!
