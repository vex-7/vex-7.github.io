Description

Experience the Thrill of Dragon Life in Dragon Simulator 3D

Enter the fantastical world of dragons with Dragon Simulator 3D, a captivating simulation game where you take on the majestic form of a dragon. Immerse yourself in this epic adventure, perfectly suited for Chromebook computers, offering seamless gameplay in web browsers with full-screen mode and an ad-free experience throughout.

Embark on Epic Dragon Quests

In Dragon Simulator 3D, your objective is to complete thrilling missions scattered across the levels and collect stars to enhance your dragon's abilities. As you gather enough stars, your dragon will ascend to the next level, growing stronger and faster with each upgrade. Engage in exciting missions that not only entertain but also hone your dragon skills, from flying over towering mountains to diving down steep peaks.

How to Play:

  • Dragons are not easy to control:
  • Move around - WASD
  • Spit fire - Left Mouse Button (LMB)
  • Attack with your tail - Right Mouse Button (RMB)
  • Take-off/Land - Q
  • Fly up - Spacebar
  • Fly down - C
  • Speed up - Shift
  • Lock cursor - L
  • Hide interface - H
