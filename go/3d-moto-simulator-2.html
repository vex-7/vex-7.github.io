Description

Get Ready for 3D Moto Simulator 2!

Jump into the adrenaline-fueled world of motorcycle racing with 3D Moto Simulator 2! Feel the rush as you speed through thrilling courses on your choice of motorcycles, including street racers, motocross bikes, and even police motorcycles. Experience the exhilarating sensation of high-speed riding with the first-person view option, and show off your skills with impressive stunts like wheelies and bunny-hops!

How to Play

Master the controls to dominate the road:

  • Use the WASD keys to drive your motorcycle, navigating through challenging terrain and obstacles with precision.
  • Switch between different bikes by pressing the 1, 2, or 3 keys, each offering its own unique characteristics and performance.
  • Activate the handbrake with the Spacebar to execute sharp turns and drifts, showcasing your mastery of motorcycle handling.
  • Experiment with various camera views by pressing the C key, allowing you to enjoy different perspectives as you race.
  • Shift gears on your bike using the Shift key, optimizing your speed and acceleration for maximum performance.
  • Reset the game or your bike at any time with the R or G keys, ensuring a seamless and uninterrupted gaming experience.
  • Engage the police lights on the police bike by pressing the E key, adding an extra layer of excitement to your races.

Features

Experience the following exciting features in 3D Moto Simulator 2:

  • Choose from a selection of three awesome bikes, including a police bike equipped with a siren for added thrills.
  • Explore three different maps, each offering unique environments and challenges to conquer.
  • Test your driving skills on challenging offroad tracks and expansive maps, providing endless opportunities for exploration and adventure.
  • Roam freely in large maps, allowing you to discover hidden secrets and thrilling landmarks as you ride.

About the Creator

3D Moto Simulator 2 was developed by Faramel Games in October 2016. Dive into the heart-pounding world of motorcycle racing and experience the excitement firsthand!
