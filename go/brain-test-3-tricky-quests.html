Description

Embark on Brain-Teasing Adventures with Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests

Join the adventure in Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests, a captivating puzzle game designed to challenge your mind, created by Unico Studio. Perfectly optimized for Chromebook computers, this game offers seamless gameplay in full screen mode without any pesky interruptions from ads.

Explore Creative Puzzles and Colorful Characters

Dive into a world of imagination with dozens of tricky questions and puzzles, each accompanied by original stories and vibrant characters. In this third installment of the brain exercise series, you'll encounter thrilling adventures where you must outsmart criminals, evade henchmen, fly planes, brew potions, confront mummies, and much more. Get ready to think outside the box and use every element on the screen as a clue to solve these creative riddles!

How to Play Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests

Master the game's mechanics with these simple controls:

  • Use your mouse cursor or finger to select, drag, and move objects around.

About the Creator

Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests is crafted by Unico Studio, a renowned game development studio based in the United States, committed to delivering engaging puzzle experiences.

Features and Gameplay

  • Levels: Enjoy over 40 challenging levels, plus discover several secret bonus levels.
  • Hints: Need a little help? Click or tap on the magnifying glass icon to reveal hints tailored for each level.
  • Skip Levels: Stuck on a level? Simply click or tap on the rewarded video icon at the bottom of the page to skip it.

Meet Alyx, the Heroine

Follow the journey of Alyx, the courageous protagonist of Brain Test 3: Tricky Quests, as she embarks on a quest to retrieve the six power gems to save her ailing father.
