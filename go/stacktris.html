Description

Enter the captivating world of Stacktris, an addictive arcade game that challenges you to stack and balance tetrominoes without toppling the tower. Combining the classic Tetris experience with the thrill of tower stacking games, Stacktris will put your balancing skills to the ultimate test.

But that's not all! Stacktris now offers the option to play in full-screen mode, immersing you completely in the heart-pounding action for an enhanced gaming experience. Plus, you can now enjoy Stacktris directly on your Chromebook Browser, ensuring seamless gameplay across various devices. And here's the best part: Say farewell to annoying ads that disrupt your concentration, as Stacktris is proudly ad-free, allowing you to focus solely on mastering your stacking skills.

In Stacktris, you'll face a constant challenge as the tetromino block spins exponentially faster. Tap on the block to halt its spin and drag to position it above the platform, then release to drop it into place. Exercise caution, as losing even a single block spells game over. But fear not! Stacktris offers hints and the ability to purchase upgrades with the coins you've collected.

Discover a range of power-ups such as Slow Spin, High Friction, Coin Magnet, and more, designed to enhance your gameplay and make the experience even more enjoyable. With patience and dedication, you'll find Stacktris to be an exhilarating experience like no other.

Challenge your friends and showcase your stacking prowess as you strive to build the tallest Tetromino Tower. Are you ready to rise to the top of the leaderboard and prove your skills in Stacktris? The challenge awaits!

How to play Stacktris?

Desktop

Click and hold to stop the tetrominos from spinning, drag your mouse to move the tetrominoes, and release to drop. You can also use the keyboard to move them horizontally. Stack the blocks as high as you can without knocking them down!

  • Stop tetrominoes - Left mouse click
  • Move tetrominoes - A / D or Left / Right arrow keys
  • Drop tetrominoes - Release left mouse click
  • Menu - ESC
  • Pause - Enter / Return

Mobile / Tablet

Long-press to stop the tetrominos from spinning, drag horizontally to position them as you lke, and release to drop. Stack the blocks as high as you can without knocking them down!
