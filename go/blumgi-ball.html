Description

Get ready for Blumgi Ball, an awesome game you can play right in your Chromebook computer browser! And guess what? It has a full-screen mode, so you can dive into the action without any distractions from ads! In this game, you're aiming to get the ball through the hoop, just like in classic basketball. But here's the cool part – you use a slingshot system to launch the ball. Pull back the indicator to set the intensity, then release to shoot! But wait, there's more excitement – you also have the power to teleport right next to your ball! This means you can pull off amazing chain combos and dunk in style! Each level you beat earns you points to unlock new characters. Get ready to explore vibrant levels full of surprises in Blumgi Ball – it's a blast!

How to play Blumgi Ball?

Get the ball through the net using a slingshot system where you pull the indicator in accordance with the intensity at which you want to throw the ball, and release it to throw.

Aim and shoot - Left mouse click, drag, and release

Teleport to ball - Space bar

How many characters does Blumgi Ball have?

There are 9 unique Blumgi Ball characters in the game so far. They are all designed after animals.
