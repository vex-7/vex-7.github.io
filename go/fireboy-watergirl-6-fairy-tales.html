Description

Welcome to Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales!

Embark on an enchanting journey in Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales, the sixth installment of the captivating platformer series. Join Fireboy and Watergirl as they explore a mystical temple filled with fairies, adding a magical twist to the familiar puzzle-solving gameplay.

Game Instructions

Teamwork:
Just like its predecessors, Fireboy and Watergirl: Fairy Tales emphasizes the power of teamwork. Partner up with a friend for an optimal 2-player experience, collaborating to solve puzzles and navigate through the temple's challenges without falling into traps.

Temples:
Embark on a journey through 31 captivating levels, including 6 special stages waiting to be unlocked. Collect diamonds corresponding to each character and make your way to the exit door in each temple challenge to progress further in the game.

New Content:
Discover the unique theme of Fireboy and Watergirl 6, featuring fairies that aid you in your quest. Utilize these magical companions by dragging them with your mouse to open doors and operate elevators, all without needing to move the main characters!

Game Tips:
Utilize fairies strategically to navigate through levels efficiently. Maintain a steady pace to avoid restarting from the beginning. For enhanced enjoyment, play with a friend to tackle the challenges together. Ensure both players reach the end to complete each level successfully.

Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales offers seamless gameplay in the browser, optimized for Chromebook computers with fullscreen mode and an ad-free experience. Join Fireboy and Watergirl in their magical adventure today!

Release Date: September 2021

Developer: Oslo Albet

Platform

Web browser (desktop and mobile)

Controls

Drag the left mouse button to move the fairies and the screen

Fireboy Controls:
Left and right arrow keys = move
Up arrow key = jump

Watergirl Controls:
AD = move
W = jump
