Description

Welcome to Duo Vikings, the thrilling cooperative adventure by 7Spot Games! Join forces with two valiant Vikings as they embark on a daring raid through obstacle-filled castle levels. Together, you'll navigate through intricate puzzles and obstacles, unlocking treasures and riches hidden within the majestic halls.

Team up with a friend to conquer each challenge, stepping on triggers, opening doors, activating elevators, and smashing breakable objects along the way. Dive into this stunning Viking world and prove your worthiness for a place in Valhalla!

Experience seamless gameplay on your Chromebook computer browser, complete with full-screen mode and an uninterrupted journey free from ads. Are you ready to unleash your inner warrior and claim victory alongside your fellow Viking? Prepare for an epic quest in Duo Vikings!

Controls:

Move - A/D or Left/Right arrow keys

Jump - W or Up arrow key

Hit/Use - S or Down arrow key
