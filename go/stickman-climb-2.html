Description

Get ready to climb to new heights in Stickman Climb 2, a super cool game you can play right in your Chromebook computer browser! And guess what? It has a full-screen mode, so you can focus on climbing without any distractions from ads! In this game, you control a stickman and your goal is to balance yourself using a pickaxe and reach the flag at the end of each level. But watch out! There are dangerous traps and spikes along the way, so be careful and use the checkpoints wisely. To add some flair to your stickman, you can unlock new colors, vessels, and weapons to customize your avatar. And the best part? You can play Stickman Climb 2 by yourself or challenge a friend to a race locally! So, get ready to climb, customize, and conquer in Stickman Climb 2!

How to play Stickman Climb 2?

Use your axe to move around the stage. Reach the flag to finish it.

Player 1

Move - A / D

Respawn - R

Player 2

Move - Left / Right arrow keys

Respawn - Enter
