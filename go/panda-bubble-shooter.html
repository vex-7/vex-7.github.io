Description

Embark on a Bubble-Bursting Adventure with Panda: Bubble Shooter!

Join the adorable world of Panda: Bubble Shooter, a captivating bubble shooting puzzle game crafted by LAK Games. As the guardian of a lovable panda family, your mission is to rescue your little ones by navigating through a series of challenging levels packed with vibrant bubbles. Utilize your precision aiming skills to pop matching bubbles and leverage an array of power-ups to aid your journey.

Immerse yourself in the panda-parenting experience as you collect coins, power-ups, treasure chests, and daily rewards. To add excitement to your adventure, take a spin on the Wheel of Fortune mini-game for additional surprises. Remember, the golden rule of parenthood is to burst the bubble and ensure your cubs' safety!

How to Play Panda: Bubble Shooter?

Master the art of bubble bursting with these simple steps:

  • Hold down the left mouse button or your finger to aim
  • Release to shoot
  • Match three or more bubbles of the same color to burst them
  • Utilize the Long Arrow booster power-up to tackle challenging areas

About the Creator

Panda: Bubble Shooter is lovingly crafted by LAK Games, a renowned developer dedicated to delivering engaging and addictive puzzle experiences. Dive into this bubbly world and embark on a delightful journey with Panda and its adorable family!
