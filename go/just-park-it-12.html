Description

Hey kids! Get ready for Just Park It 12, the twelfth part of the awesome car parking series! In this game, you'll drive a huge truck through tricky streets packed with obstacles and busy traffic. Your challenge? Navigate through the traffic without crashing the truck, and then park it perfectly in the right spot! Each level is like a real-life parking situation, so get ready for some serious parking skills practice. Can you show off your parking prowess and prove that you're the best at parking vehicles? And guess what? You can play Just Park It 12 on your Chromebook computer browser, and you can even make it full-screen for the ultimate gaming experience! Plus, there are no annoying ads to bother you while you play. So, let's hit the road and park like a pro!

How to play:

Park your vehicle without losing all your health.
Brake - Space key
Drive - Arrow keys
