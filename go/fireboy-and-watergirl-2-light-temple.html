Description

Welcome to Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple!

Embark on an illuminating adventure in Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple, the second installment of the cooperative platformer series. Delve into a world where light and reflections hold the key to overcoming challenging puzzles across 40 exhilarating levels!

How to Play

Play cooperatively:
Team up with a friend for the ultimate gaming experience! Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple is designed for cooperative play, allowing two players to control the characters using the same keyboard. Work together to navigate the labyrinthine puzzles and conquer each level.

Harness your powers:
Utilize the unique strengths of Fireboy and Watergirl, along with the power of teamwork, to overcome obstacles. With 40 levels to conquer, strategic cooperation is essential to tackle the increasing challenges that await.

Use light to your advantage:
Manipulate light and reflections to progress through the Light Temple. Employ mirrors to direct beams of light towards buttons and devices, unlocking doors and activating elevators. Master the use of light to pave your way to victory!

The Fireboy and Watergirl Series

Discover more cooperative adventures in the Fireboy and Watergirl series:

  • Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 4: Crystal Temple
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Features

  • Play with a friend for cooperative fun
  • Conquer 40 challenging stages in the Light Temple
  • Utilize light and mirrors to solve puzzles

Release Date

Originally released as a Flash game in October 2010, Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple was updated with HTML5 technology in July 2018, ensuring compatibility with modern web browsers.

Developer

Developed by Oslo Albet, Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple promises hours of cooperative entertainment!

Platform

This game is optimized for web browsers, making it suitable for Chromebook computers. Enjoy fullscreen mode and uninterrupted gameplay without ads!

Controls

Fireboy Controls: Arrow keys
Watergirl Controls: WASD
