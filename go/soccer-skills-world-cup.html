Description

Hey everyone! Get ready for Soccer Skills World Cup, a super cool 3D sports game where you can play exciting soccer tournaments right from your Chromebook computer browser! You can jump into fast-paced matches against the computer or real players and guide your team to victory! Pick your favorite national team and compete in the quarter-finals, semi-finals, and the big championship finale! Whether you call it soccer or football, this game is sure to test your skills and put you in the heart of the action. Just drag your finger or mouse to move your player and kick the ball. The harder you drag, the faster you'll go and the stronger your kicks will be. Plus, you can do throw-ins, corner kicks, fouls, and penalties! So, are you ready to become the world's greatest football team? Let's kick off and find out! And guess what? No ads will interrupt your game!

How can I play Soccer Skills World Cup?

Drag your finger our mouse towards the direction you want to move. When you let go, you will kick the ball. The intensity of your drag will affect your speed as well as your power.

Who created Soccer Skills World Cup?

Soccer Skills World Cup is a sports game created by Radical Play.
