Description

Experience Futuristic Racing in Cyber Cars Punk Racing

Enter the adrenaline-fueled world of Cyber Cars Punk Racing, a cutting-edge 3D driving game set in a sky-high futuristic metropolis. Take the wheel, ignite your nitro, and race through neon-lit streets as you strive to outpace your rivals and emerge victorious. With full compatibility on web browsers, especially optimized for Chromebook computers, enjoy seamless gameplay with no ads to interrupt your racing experience.

Master the Controls

Get ready to dominate the streets with these controls:

  • Player 1:
  • Drive - WASD or Arrow keys
  • Nitro - N
  • Change camera - C
  • Player 2:
  • Drive - WASD or Arrow keys
  • Nitro - T

About Cyber Cars Punk Racing

Developed by AYN Games, Cyber Cars Punk Racing offers an immersive racing experience with various game modes including Career, Hot Chase, Battle Arena, and Free Ride. Collect money, complete missions, and unlock an array of skins, upgrades, and new cars to customize your ride. Challenge your need for speed and dominate the cyber town's racing scene!
