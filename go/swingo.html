Description

Welcome to Swingo!

Embark on a thrilling arcade adventure with Swingo, a captivating game that seamlessly blends platforming and action elements into one delightful experience. Take control of a charming bouncy character equipped with a grappling hook, and swing your way through a series of vibrant levels filled with surprises!

How to Play

In Swingo, mastering the grappling hook is key to navigating the colorful landscapes. To move around, simply aim your grappling hook using the left mouse button, drag it in the direction you want to go, and release to fire. Your character will swing and bounce until you reach the tantalizing fruit at the end of each level. Can you master the art of swinging to progress?

New Characters and Unlockables

Discover a myriad of adorable characters waiting to be unlocked in Swingo! From playful frogs to majestic animals, each character brings its own unique charm to the game. Collect points by successfully completing levels and unlock these delightful creatures to accompany you on your swinging adventures!

About the Creator

Swingo is the brainchild of Blumgi, a talented game developer known for creating engaging and entertaining gaming experiences. Prepare to embark on an unforgettable journey filled with swinging action and adorable characters as you dive into the world of Swingo!
