Description

Welcome to Gunspin!

Embark on an exciting journey in Gunspin, a thrilling physics game where you aim to keep your gun airborne for as long as possible! Upgrade your firearm with more bullets and power to propel it even farther into the skies.

How to Play Gunspin

Take aim and fire your gun to the left, using all your bullets to launch the gun as far as you can. Every meter you travel earns you valuable coins. Use these coins to purchase more bullets, enhance your gun's power, and acquire ammo boxes to extend your journey.

Unlock new firearms by reaching distance milestones during each round. Each gun boasts unique sounds and stats, allowing you to find the perfect match to maximize your score!

Features

Experience the addictive thrill of traveling the furthest distance as you strive for mastery.

Challenge your friends to beat your high score and become the ultimate Gunspin champion.

Unlock a variety of guns, each with its own distinctive stats, and discover your favorite to dominate the skies.

Upgrade your firearms to reach even greater distances and achieve new heights of success.

Release Date

Gunspin was released in November 2019, offering players an exhilarating journey through the skies.

Platform

Enjoy Gunspin directly in your web browser, making it easily accessible on various platforms, including Chromebook computers.

Controls

Simply press the left mouse button to shoot and propel your gun into the air, setting the stage for an unforgettable adventure!
