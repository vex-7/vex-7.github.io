Description

Welcome to Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple!

Embark on an icy adventure in Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple, the third installment of the beloved cooperative platformer series. Explore the frosty depths of the ice temple, sliding your way through tricky puzzles with the help of your friend or through solo mastery. With 36 stages of increasing difficulty, teamwork and strategic thinking are key to mastering this frozen challenge.

Game Instructions

Play cooperatively:
Join forces with a friend or tackle the challenge solo by controlling both Fireboy and Watergirl. Utilize the same keyboard for cooperative play, maneuvering through icy labyrinths and solving puzzles together. Alternatively, take on the challenge solo, controlling both characters to navigate the frozen depths of the temple.

Utilize your strengths:
Harness the unique abilities of Fireboy and Watergirl, along with the power of teamwork, to overcome each tricky situation. With the addition of icy terrain, strategic planning becomes even more critical. Think creatively to navigate through slippery situations and conquer the frozen obstacles that lie ahead.

Solve problems:
Navigate through a variety of puzzles featuring elements such as water, lava, slime, and now ice and light as you progress through the game. Work collaboratively with your partner to unravel the increasingly complex challenges that await you in the ice temple. Put your problem-solving skills to the test as you traverse through the frozen depths.

The Fireboy and Watergirl Series

Discover the full series of Fireboy and Watergirl games, each offering unique cooperative challenges:

  • Fireboy and Watergirl 1: Forest Temple
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 4: Crystal Temple
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements
  • Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Features

  • Control both characters solo or with a friend
  • 36 stages of complex puzzles to conquer
  • Strategic use of ice that affects both players differently

Release Date

Originally released as a Flash game in July 2014, Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple was updated to HTML5 in September 2018, ensuring compatibility with modern web browsers on both desktop and mobile platforms.

Developer

Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple is developed by Oslo Albet.

Platforms

Enjoy seamless gameplay on web browsers, including desktop and mobile platforms.

Controls

Watergirl controls:
AD to move
W to jump

Fireboy controls:
Left and right arrow keys to move
Up arrow key to jump
